Enhance Your Fitness Journey

Elevate your workouts and achieve your fitness goals with our tailored exercise plans.

Begin your fitness journey by customizing your workout schedule. Choose the number of days you wish to dedicate to the gym and select from our curated list of exercises. Then, effortlessly track your progress as you work towards your fitness goals.

Types of Workout

Push Workout

Incorporate these push exercises into your workout routine to target muscles such as chest, shoulders, and triceps. Aim for 3 sets of 8-12 reps for each exercise with a 60-90 seconds rest between sets:

  • Bench Press
  • Shoulder Press
  • Tricep Dips
  • Push-ups
  • Incline Dumbbell Press

Pull Workout

Add these pull exercises to your routine to work on muscles like back, biceps, and rear delts. Perform 3 sets of 8-12 reps for each exercise with a 60-90 seconds rest between sets:

  • Deadlifts
  • Pull-ups
  • Bent-over Rows
  • Face Pulls
  • Lat Pulldowns

Legs Workout

These leg exercises will help you build strength and definition in your lower body. Perform 3 sets of 10-15 reps for each exercise with a 60-90 seconds rest between sets:

  • Squats
  • Lunges
  • Leg Press
  • Deadlifts
  • Calf Raises

Upper Body Workout

Target your upper body muscles with these effective exercises. Aim for 3 sets of 8-12 reps for each exercise with a 60-90 seconds rest between sets:

  • Bench Press
  • Pull-ups
  • Shoulder Press
  • Bicep Curls
  • Tricep Extensions

Lower Body Workout

Strengthen and tone your lower body muscles with these exercises. Perform 3 sets of 10-15 reps for each exercise with a 60-90 seconds rest between sets:

  • Squats
  • Deadlifts
  • Lunges
  • Leg Press
  • Calf Raises

Cardio

Add cardio exercises to improve your cardiovascular health and burn calories. Perform 20-30 minutes of cardio, such as running, cycling, or swimming, with a 60-90 seconds rest between sets:

  • Running
  • Cycling
  • Swimming
  • Jump Rope
  • Elliptical Training
